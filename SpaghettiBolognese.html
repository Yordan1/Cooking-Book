Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  1. 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon, finely chopped
  3. 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  4. 2 carrots, trimmed and finely chopped
  5. 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  6. 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  7. 2-3 sprigs rosemary, leaves picked and finely chopped
  8. 500g beef mince

    9. For the Bolognese sauce:
  9. 2 x 400g tins plum tomatoes
  10. small pack basil, leaves picked, ¾
    finely chopped and the rest left whole for garnish
  11. 1 tsp dried oregano
  12. 2 fresh bay leaves
  13. 2 tbsp tomato purée
  14. 1 beef stock cube
  15. 1 red chilli, seeded and finely chopped (optional)
  16. 125ml red wine
  17. 6 cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
    18. To season and serve:
  18. 75g parmesan, grated, plus extra to serve
  19. 400g spaghetti
  20. crusty bread, to serve (optional)

Method:

Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.
Add the bacon and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp.
Reduce the heat and add the onion, carrot,
celery, garlic and rosemary, then fry for 10 mins.
Stir the veg often until it softens.
Increase the heat to medium-high,
add the mince and cook stirring for 3-4 mins until the meat
is browned all over.
Add the tinned tomatoes, chopped basil, oregano, bay leaves,
tomato purée, stock cube, chilli, wine and cherry tomatoes.
Stir with a wooden spoon, breaking up the plum tomatoes.
Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover with a lid.
Cook for 1 hr 15 mins stirring occasionally, until you have a rich,
thick sauce. Add the Parmesan, check the seasoning and stir.
When the Bolognese is nearly
finished cook the spaghetti following pack instructions.
Drain the spaghetti and stir into the Bolognese sauce.
Serve with grated Parmesan, the extra basil leaves and crusty bread.
